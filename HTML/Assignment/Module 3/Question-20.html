What ate logical and physical tags in HTML


logical tags :

In some contexts, "logical tags" may refer to semantic or structural HTML elements that convey the meaning or purpose of the content they enclose. These elements help define the structure of the document and its relationship to other elements

Example : <a> <abbr>


physical tags :

Conversely, "physical tags" might be interpreted as elements that affect the visual appearance or presentation of the content rather than its underlying meaning or structure.

Example : <span> <div> <b> <u> <i> <sup> <sub>

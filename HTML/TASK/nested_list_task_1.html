Business And finance :
Business and finance refer to the broad field encompassing activities related to the management, operation, and financial aspects of organizations, including businesses, corporations, and financial institutions. It involves various activities such as planning, organizing, controlling, and directing the resources and operations of a company to achieve its financial goals and objectives.

It Career field :
The IT (Information Technology) career field encompasses a wide range of roles and professions related to technology, computer systems, software development, and information management. IT professionals work with computers, networks, databases, software applications, and other technological tools to support and enhance various aspects of an organization's operations.
